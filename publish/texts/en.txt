FULL DESCRIPTION (max 1000)
Turbo Traffic 3D is a high-speed endless racer through dense traffic. Floor the gas, switch lanes and shave past cars by an inch: the bolder the overtake, the more points you score and the faster your nitro charges.

Three modes: Classic — a survival run where the oncoming lane opens up as you speed up; Sprint — 90 seconds to stack the biggest score; Rampage — one minute of total impunity, ram everything that moves.

Collect coins and unlock 12 cars: from a sport sedan to a police car, fire truck, garbage truck and a hypercar from the future. Grab powerups — magnet, shield and x2 — honk at slowpokes, dodge traffic cones and keep an eye on the sky: a UFO has been spotted over the highway at night.

A living road with a full day-night cycle — sunset, starry night, dawn and day. Missions with rewards, a daily bonus and a leaderboard. Plays with one hand, works offline.

Buckle up — the traffic won't wait!

HOW TO PLAY (max 1000)
Goal: drive as far as you can and score big without crashing into traffic.

Controls:
• On phone: tap the left/right edge of the screen or swipe to change lanes; on-screen buttons fire nitro and the horn.
• On desktop: arrows ← → or A/D steer, Space or W is nitro, G is horn, P is pause.

Points flow in with speed. A close overtake ("Near miss!") gives a bonus and builds your combo; passing in the oncoming lane pays double. Risky overtakes charge the nitro gauge: once it is full, hit the boost — while it lasts you are invincible and smash cars out of your way.

Powerups on the road: the magnet pulls in coins, the shield forgives one crash, x2 doubles your score. Spend coins in the garage on new cars.

Modes: Classic — endless run, the oncoming lane opens after 20 seconds; Sprint — 90 seconds for a record; Rampage — 60 seconds of invincibility with coins for every ram.

Tip: keep the combo alive — a streak of near misses multiplies your score, and nitro in traffic gives you both speed and protection.
